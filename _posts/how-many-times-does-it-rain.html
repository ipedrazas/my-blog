So, in case you follow me on Twitter, you'd surely have seen tweets like the one in the image, and I'm pretty sure that you could not believe that I was going to go out to run so often (I'm sorry, I actually didn't realise until it was too late what a misleading message it was. From here, I publicly apologise).

Anyway, you can see in the screenshot that yes, it rained 8 times, which is quite a lot of rain! For the record, I've disabled the IFTTT trigger so I'm not be bothering you anymore *about me getting wet in the rain, at least).
